🌿About Eric Coste

Eric Coste is a digital creator, author, and educator who believes creativity should be simple, meaningful, and free from limits. He writes books that help people build confidence, take control of their lives, and unlock their creative and financial potential one practical step at a time.

Eric’s work blends psychology, storytelling, and modern tools like AI to show that success is not about luck or perfection. It is about systems, purpose, and mindset. Whether he is teaching readers how to build faceless TikTok channels, repair credit, or master manifestation, his goal stays the same: to help everyday people create extraordinary change.

When he is not writing, Eric spends his time exploring nature, studying ancient wisdom, and building digital projects that make knowledge accessible to everyone.
